Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost pet?

Click on "Report Pet" in the navigation menu and fill out the form with as much detail as possible, including a photo.

How do I search for my lost pet?

Visit the "Find Pets" page and use the search filters to look for pets matching your pet's description.

Is the service free?

Yes! PetRescue is completely free to use. Our mission is to help reunite pets with their families.

How do I contact someone who found my pet?

Contact information is displayed on each pet's detail page. You can also submit a request through our platform.
